Smart Loan Contracts

We employ Smart Loan Contracts: a particular sort of programme that encapsulates business logic and executes on a dedicated virtual machine baked into a distributed ledger like a blockchain.

Smart Loan contracts can be automatically updated by the framework to express the patterns in low-cost ways.
